Hi,

Yes, this is technically feasible, but there are some important legal and practical considerations to be aware of.

The booking system is managed by the Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency, which has measures in place to prevent automated access and booking. Fully automated bots that log in and book appointments may violate their terms and can lead to account restrictions or blocking.

A more appropriate and reliable approach is to develop a monitoring and alert system that assists you in securing a slot while keeping final control with the user.


---

Proposed Solution Options

Option 1: Basic Monitoring Bot

Automatically refreshes the booking page at short intervals

Detects available (green) slots instantly

Highlights availability on screen

Notifies user via sound or on-screen alert

Manual booking remains with the user



---

Option 2: Enhanced Assistant Bot

Faster refresh using modern browser automation tools

Tracks multiple test centres simultaneously

Sends instant alerts via Telegram or WhatsApp

Can assist with pre-filling booking details

User completes final confirmation



---

Option 3: Advanced Automation System

High-frequency monitoring with optimized performance

Multi-session tracking for different centres

Proxy support to reduce blocking risk

Semi-automated booking assistance

Designed to compete with existing high-speed systems



---

Recommended Approach

The most practical and compliant solution is Option 2 (Enhanced Assistant Bot). It significantly improves your chances of securing a slot while minimizing the risk of being blocked.

Yes, it’s definitely possible to build a bot that monitors for the earliest available dates (whether next week or next month) and instantly clicks “View” when a slot appears, but fully automatic reservation is a bit risky because the system can detect bots and may block your account or trigger captchas. A safer and more reliable approach is to use session cookies (so you don’t have to share your password), let the bot continuously monitor and instantly open or pre-fill the booking page, and then you manually confirm the reservation — this way you’re still extremely fast while avoiding most of the risks.
